About the studio

An Independent Post‑Production Boutique

Blacomix is an independent digital art, animation and film brand — a self‑employed post‑production hub built in Kolkata and run by artist and animator Koushik Karar.

The studio crafts cinematic visuals and virtual worlds using industry tools like Maya, Blender and Arnold, moving between commercial VFX, 3D environments, and digital art collections released independently, including work hosted on OpenSea.

Its debut short, Dark Night Don (2019), set the tone the studio still works from — motion built to carry feeling, not just spectacle.

"Every frame has to earn its emotion before it earns its motion."
— Koushik Karar, Founder & Creative Head
